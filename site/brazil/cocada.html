Cocada

Brazil’s iconic chewy coconut sweet — tropical, soft, and irresistibly rich.

Brazilian Dessert Coconut • Sweet Serves 10–12

Traditional Brazilian Coconut Candy

Cocada is a classic Brazilian delicacy made from coconut, sugar, and condensed milk. Rich, chewy, and tropical — it's a quick recipe that feels festive and indulgent.

Prep10 mins
Cook15 mins
DifficultyEasy
Tropical Quick Sweet Traditional
Ingredients
  • 3 cups shredded coconut
  • 1½ cups sugar
  • 1 cup condensed milk
  • ½ cup water
  • ½ tsp vanilla essence
  • Optional: pinch of salt
Variations
  • Chocolate Cocada — add 2 tbsp cocoa
  • Toasted Cocada — lightly toast coconut first
  • Brown Cocada — swap sugar for brown sugar
Method
  1. Heat sugar + water until dissolved.
  2. Add coconut and cook while stirring.
  3. Add condensed milk and reduce until thick.
  4. Add vanilla or optional flavors.
  5. Shape into scoops or spread on a tray.
  6. Cool 20–30 minutes before serving.
Storage

Keep in an airtight container for up to 5 days. Do not refrigerate to maintain softness.
